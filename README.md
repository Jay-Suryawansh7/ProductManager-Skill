# ProductManager-Skill

A top 1% Product Manager skill for AI assistants (like Claude, Antigravity, or other agents). This skill transforms your AI from a simple coder into a high-level PM that owns product outcomes, strategy, and reality.

## 🚀 Overview

The **ProductManager Skill** is designed to stop feature-first thinking and refocus development on user problems and business value. It doesn't just build what you ask; it pressure-tests your ideas, defines real user segments, and creates a full suite of execution-ready documents.

### Key Capabilities

- **Pressure-Testing**: Validate problem specificity and business viability before writing a single line of code.
- **PRD Generation**: Create PRDs with clear acceptance criteria and traceable metrics.
- **Strategy & GTM**: Build deep strategy docs and Go-To-Market plans (with a specific focus on supply-side acquisition for marketplaces).
- **Execution Roadmaps**: Realistic, Go/No-Go based roadmaps with risk registers.

## 📁 Project Structure

- **`SKILL.md`**: The core logic and operational roadmap for the AI assistant.
- **`references/`**:
    - `doc-templates.md`: Templates for all 10 product documents.
    - `pm-frameworks.md`: Reference material for RICE, JTBD, AARRR, and more.

## 🛠️ Setup & Installation

To use this skill with your AI assistant:

1. **Clone the Repository**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Jay-Suryawansh7/ProductManager-Skill.git
   ```

2. **Register the Skill**:
   Depending on your AI environment, you might need to:
   - Point your assistant's skill/plugin directory to this folder.
   - Or simply provide `SKILL.md` as context when starting a product-focused project.

3. **Usage**:
   Trigger the skill by mentioning "product", "feature", "idea", "roadmap", "MVP", or "metrics". The AI will immediately shift into PM-CEO mode.
